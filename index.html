Kidoos - Project Report

Duration: 29th September - 3rd October, 2025

Developed by:Vetri IT Systems Pvt Ltd

Executive Summary

Kidoos Learning is a modern, web-based educational platform designed to provide engaging and interactive online learning experiences for kids. The platform is built to deliver high-quality digital education with a focus on fun, accessibility, and child-friendly design. It enables parents to explore courses, enroll children, and stay updated with their learning progress.
